About Our Personalised Kids

Now let your kids stand apart with high quality designer accessories that displays their name on it! Enjoy the excitement before the new school/college term by customizing these hand-crafted kids accessories. Whether you’re buying for a toddler, school/college going kids or simply seeking gift ideas for kids amongst family and friends, Zoomin offers a carefully curated selection of thoughtful gifts for kids of all age groups. For photograph-based customized gifts, go for the themed Photo Cushion and 56-pieced Photo Jigsaw Puzzle. Your little one will love to create a puzzle that surprises them with their favourite photograph. Add your family picture on our adorable little Photo Cushions for the best birthday gift. Best of all, you can create these straight from your device, Facebook, Instagram, Google photos or our App. This truly makes it an easy way to gift meaningfully to nieces, nephews, or even your own children. For unique personalized gifts for toddlers, young kids and college-goers, Zoomin offers you high quality eco-friendly Bags & Pouches. You can pick from a huge variety of themes as per the kids preference. Add their name or any text of your choice – voila, the perfect birthday gift, return gift, personalized birthday favours & more!

How to create them

To create your Photo Cushion, first pick the theme that you want to go for. Next upload the photograph (it can be your personal photo, a pinterest pin, a collage, any artwork or even something that you create on Canva.com etc!). After you finish editing and finalizing the photograph, hit on Add to Cart and make the payment. Similarly, for creating your Photo Puzzle, upload the photograph, adjust it as you’d like and hit Add to Cart. For creating any of the Bags & Pouches, select the theme that you’d like first. Then add the desired text. Once you have cross checked the preview, click on Add to Cart and make payment.

Details of our variety of Kids

  • Photo Puzzle: Comes in a size of 8”x10” with 56 pieces of high quality acrylic material + a photo print included
  • Photo Cushion: 11” Square cushion with customized cover. Made in Canva Polyduck material. Pick from 11 themes + 1 only photo option.
  • Drawstring Bag: Eco-friendly 220 GSM natural cotton material. Bag size is 13.5”x16” with a 8mm cotton string.
  • Catchall Pouch: Eco-friendly 315 GSM natural cotton material. Pouch size is 9.75”x6” with an antique zipper & slider
