About Our Personalised Gifts

WE Framed it!’s Stay organized in style or give a gift that lasts all year-round – our range of custom photo calendars includes a variety of designs perfect for wall or tabletop display. From classic, sleek to more fun-loving - you’ll be sure to find designer theme options to suit your style.

How to create them

Why bother stepping out to hunt for boring & common gifts when you can order personalized ones from the comforts of your home!? Simply select the gifts you want from our variety of types of Mugs, Magnets & Bagtags. Select a theme you like & upload your pictures. You can edit and/or replace images in the next screen. Hit on print and relax. Your gift will reach you or your loved ones doorstep shortly! You can add an additional gift wrap + personal note card at checkout.

Details of our variety of Gifts

  • Select from exciting goodies like Magnets, Bagtags & 4 different Mug styles
  • Magnets: You can pick from over 70 cute themes & add upto 5 pictures. These magnets come in a friendly size of 3.75”x5.5” & are printed on 340 GMS flexible imported material
  • Bagtags: Select from 50 adorable themes & add an image + text of your choice. The bagtags come in a handy size of 1.70”x3.5”. Printed on high quality water-proof material, they also include a loop for easy attachment on any bags.
  • Coffee Mugs: Pick from 4 exciting varieties – Mug (Black), Mug (White), Colour inside Mug & Magic Mug (image will show up when hot liquid is poured in the mug!)
  • Pick designer themes & upload your favourite pictures or quotes on these mugs
