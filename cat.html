About Our Personalised Photobooks

WE Framed it!’s Photobooks are a sleek & modern version of the old school ‘Photo albums’. Instead of printing your photos and THEN making an album/scrapbook our Photobooks allow you to directly upload your pictures, design the layout, add captions and print them into a beautiful book full of bright pictures.

How to create them

Nervous about creating your Photobook online? There is absolutely no reason to be. We have taken utmost care to keep the process super simple for anyone to create photobooks with ease. All you have to do is select the theme of your choice & upload your pictures through our website or app. After that you can edit each page layout to add more pictures (except for the 5.5”x5.5” size), change the image orientation, edit the image, add text and cute little stickers – customization meets perfection! You can even sync your pictures directly through google photos, facebook or instgram. If you want to make multiple Photobooks with little or no changes then just save your creation before proceeding to checkout. You can easily duplicate and/or edit it in the creations tab.

Details of our variety of Photobooks

  • Pick from 4 size options – 5.5x5.5”, 8x8”, 8x10”, 10x8”
  • Select from a softcover or a hardcover option
  • Pick either Premium Matte or Premium Glossy page quality
  • Start with 20 or 32 pages & add more pictures as you customize each page
  • Choose from 95+ designer themes to suit each occasion
  • Edit the layout of each page, add text and stickers wherever you want

Best suited for

The best about these personalized Photobooks is that they are fully customizable, of excellent quality and thus make for the best Photo gifts & keepsakes!

  • Create a couples love story as an anniversary gift
  • Brighten up anyone’s birthday with a few unique pictures and stickers
  • Re-live each and every travel, trip, vacation and preserve those memories into Photobooks
  • Capture your pre wedding photoshoots, baby photoshoots, pre-natal/pregnancy photoshoots in our designer themed photobooks
  • Easily create coffee table books, lookbooks, designer display books for all your personal & professional needs
  • Document your child’s growth story and create a perfect keepsake
  • Birthday celebrations, festive celebrations, anniversary parties, get-togethers, engagement parties, sangeet night – create a photobook for every special day
  • For your wedding pictures, our stunning LayFlat Photo Album is the perfect fit!
