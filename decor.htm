About Our Personalised Décor

WE Framed it!’s Discover these chic decor options that are sure to breathe a new life into any room! Whether you’re looking to gift someone on a special occasion or planning for festive gifts for your family or just want a fresh look for your old walls – our range of customized decor options like Canvas Prints, Metal Prints, Enlargement & Collage Posters got your covered

How to create them

If artistic look walls overwhelm you then you should know this – creating customized decor with We framed it can be done in 3 steps and within a few minutes! Did we mention that you can finish this completely online with the product reaching your doorsteps within a few days. Could it get easier than this? Pick the decor you want to customize from the four decor products. Simply select the size & theme of your choice. You will be able to edit your creation on the next screen and add text wherever applicable. Review your final product & hit print!

Details of our variety of Décor

  • • Canvas Prints – Pick from 5 majestic sizes & 50 designer themes. Your product will reach you Mounted on 1.75’’ thick wooden frame with white borders across & ready to hang!
  • • Metal Prints – Pick from 5 apt sizes & choose from a variety of themes. Your Metal print will include a ready-to-display 3mm frosted acrylic stand
  • • Enlargements – Pick from 4 large size options. Frame them up or display using our wall-safe washi tape!
  • • Collage Posters – Available in 4 sizes, these posters are printed on high quality Fujifilm Matte photo paper. The perfect gift on a budget!
