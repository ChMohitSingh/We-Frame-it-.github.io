About Our Personalised Calendars

WE Framed it!’s Photobooks are a sleek & modern version of the old school ‘Photo albums’. Instead of printing your photos and THEN making an album/scrapbook our Photobooks allow you to directly upload your pictures, design the layout, add captions and print them into a beautiful book full of bright pictures.

How to create them

Wondering how to create an adorable photo calendar filled with your perfect moments? We’ve made it super easy and convenient for you! All you have to do is pick a theme that suits your style & upload your photos. You can edit your picture & replace it for each page. In the next screen you can select which month you want the calendar to start from & even swap pictures. Add your final creation to the cart and you’re done!

Details of our variety of Calendars

  • You have 7 types of calendars to select from! 3 sizes of desktop calendars, 3 types of wall calendars and even a magnet calendar!
  • Pick from 50 professionally designed themes
  • Have them placed on any table top or hung on any wall!
  • Best part about our wall-mount calendar? Tear away the perforated photos on every page to reduce paper waste and give your photos a second life.

Best suited for

The best about these personalized Photobooks is that they are fully customizable, of excellent quality and thus make for the best Photo gifts & keepsakes!

  • Our calendars are a great birthday gift for someone who has everything!
  • Gift your work-obsessed friend/sibling to add a smile to their face every day
  • These make for great anniversary gifts for your better half – just upload your wedding/engagement/pre-wedding photoshoot/honeymoon pictures!
  • Surprise a graduating boy/girl with our wall mount calendar that doubles up as photo prints at the end of the month!
