About Our Personalised Framed Prints

Say goodbye to dull walls, and hello to customized Photo Frames. Create vibrant gallery walls or a just a single table top Frame. Pick from 80+ variety of sizes and finishes, specifically designed to make your favourite space look its best, while displaying your own framed prints.

How to create them

Creating a photo frame has never been easier. No need to step out to hunt for Frames, match the correct sized picture & go back again to collect the final product! With Zoomin’s range of Framed Prints, simply upload your pictures, select the frames and receive the final Photo Frame (prints included!) at the click of a few buttons. After browsing from our range of Premium Frames, Classic Frames, Coloured Frames & Basic Frames- click on the frame type & select the desired size. Now upload your photo/s and proceed to edit the image. Depending on the frame type, you will be given the option to select the frame finish/colour & change your mind about the size as well.

Details of our variety of Framed Prints

  • Pick from four broad categories: Basic Framed Prints, Classic Framed Prints, Coloured Framed Prints & Premium Framed Prints
  • You can select different size options depending on the frame finish you opt for. Pick either landscape or portrait orientation.
  • After uploading your image/s you can edit them in the next screen.
  • In case of our stunning Premium Framed Prints, you can select from 50 adorable themes, upload upto 8 pictures & also add text!
  • The final Frame will be delivered with a high quality print inside + it comes ready to display.
